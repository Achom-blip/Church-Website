About Us

Christ Summit Ministries is a Christ-centered church dedicated to spreading the Gospel and transforming lives through faith, love, and service.

Our Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ and empower them to live purposeful lives.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope, raising a generation grounded in faith and committed to impacting the world positively.
